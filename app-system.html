App System

Range Time: 21h-5h & 12h-14h

6.1. Range Time Push-Up to Earn
The system has 2 default range time: 21p.m to 5a.m and 12p.m to 14p.m
If you Push-Up in these two time-frames, your earn coefficient will be 1. That means you will not get deducted when Pushing-Up on time.
If you push-up outside of these two time-frames, you will have an earn coefficient of: 0,5, which means you will be divided in half the money you earn
So, in order to optimize, Push-Up at the right time.

6.2. Energy Account
This stat is the total energy used during the push-up, the minimum is 0 and the maximum is 100.
- If your energy reaches 0, your Push-Up will not earn a token.
- Energy will be deducted whenever you wake up with a coefficient that can be 5 minutes of push-up will subtract a certain amount of energy.
- Energy recovery is that when you wake up, energy will recover back to a maximum of 100
- Energy recovery process can be increased by NFT Pillow.

6.3. Deep Push-up
This is the number of minutes of deep push-up you get to use. This stat will be accumulated from the NFTs that you use. When you turn on deep Push-Up mode, your PTE earn tokens will increase by a certain percentage, this rate will be adjusted according to the Pool's adjustment every month and every season of the year. But it will help you to earn many Tokens. This mode is only used per day but does not accumulate. If it is not used up in the previous day, it will be reset.
For example:
Deep push-up from Bed: 30’
Deep push-up from Pillow: 25’
Deep Push-Up from Blanket: 22’
Rate: 1,2
⇨ Total deep Push-Up: 30 + 25 + 22= 77 minutes
Therefore, when you choose deep Push-Up mode, in addition to the earned default value, you will be raised by 1.2 times the amount of token earned in 77 minutes.

6.4. Money Earn
This index only exists in NFT Bed. It’s the number of tokens you’ll earn after 5-minute Pushing-up. The more NFT Bed you have, ther more you can earn.
You can earn more tokens by strengthening stars and attaching them. Or you can use Deep push-up, Month use concurrently.
The system will record each time you complete 5-minute pushing-Up. The number must be multiple of 5
For example:
Money earn: 10
Push-Up Time: 44 minutes
Multiple of 5: 44/5 = 8.8 → 8 So money earned: 8*10 = 80 PTE

6.5. Energy Earn
This index only exists in NFT Pillow. It increases energy that is recovered when you awake and round it up to 0.5. For example 2.5 => 3 and 2.4 => 2
Energy is very important because it helps you Push-Up more and deeper. It helps you earn more. The more you recover energy, the more activities you can take part in everyday and gain more gifts. Let’s make full advantage of it! This index can increase while you push level and star up.
For example:
Normal: 10 minutes = 1 energy point
Energy earn: 5%
Awake time: 12h => Normal = (12*60/10) + (12*60/10)*2/100 = 72+4= 78.

6.6. Reliability
All NFTs have their own reliability index. If you use NFT while Pushing-Up, reliability will decrease due to Pushing-Up time. If an NFT catches 0 reliability, it will be invalid during the earning process. So you should fix the NFT before using it to push-up. This index can increase while you push level and star up. If the reliability index is high, you can fix many things.

6.7. Month Use
This index just exists in the NFT Blanket. NFT Blanket is just available in a few months. So try to optimize it. This index can not be upgraded and the earning rate will be published at the beginning of the new month.
For example:
Month use: 7
Rate 7th month: 1.3
So in 7th month, your earning process will increase 1.3 times

6.8. Lucky Earn
This value is accumulated from all NFTs.
While you’re Pushing-Up, this lucky rate helps you get valuable gifts including: NFT, Box. FIT tokens. There will be a pool size for events every week or month. There will be lucky spins which are affected by this ratio.
This rate will increase luck especially in a special monthly event.
Own and increase this rate, the dream of a valuable property will come true.

6.9. Quest System
The task system is also an extremely important function of the system. However, we will upgrade to the mainnet version as soon as possible.
Daily tasks are extremely diverse, but the goal is to help people Push-Up better. Moreover, you will receive an extremely surprising and meaningful gift. To make the moments you use more fun and meaningful.

6.10. Anti-Cheating System
Most of the GameFi or other financial applications are always prey for hackers. So we have anti-hacking scenarios for every situation in order to minimize problems with your account.
The Fire system is large and very efficient.
The Rule App system is stricter, avoids inflation and activities that take advantage of security holes.
The system controls all transactions and warns against abnormal signs.
Specially, it ensures standard operation and helps to increase everyone's health. Do not allow hackers to make money or destroy.
We look forward to your companionship and if you find any problems, please let us know to make the product better.

6.11. Tax and Fee
We will update taxes and fees before the mainnet soon.

6.12. PTE Transfer To Money
This is the secret that the project gives to everyone. PTE is a token in the app. And you can convert it to BSUD or USDT (Depends on time) with a settled rate that will help you feel more secure so you can push-up well with the app.
